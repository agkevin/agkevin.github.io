Electric Jetta

Spring 2016

Ongoing project on making a complete transition from a ICE Jetta to 100% electric Jetta, held at my home university in Puebla. My contribuition in this project was in the brake system area.

In order to make the braking system work without the ICE, we had 2 options, replace all the system or incorporate some componentes to make the old system work. The solution I decided was to install an electric vacuum pump, to replace the vaccumm offered by the engine's manifold. This pump plus a senor will supply the necessary vaccum for the Brake Booster in the car. The Brake Booster provides a help to the driver on applying less force in the braking pedal.

The other major features in the car are:

Ultracapacitors, Inverter, Charging Port & Motor Controller.
