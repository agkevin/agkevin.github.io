Telemetry E-kart

Spring 2017

This project was developed for my exchange university in Ingolstadt, Germany. The original title of the project is „Aufbau eines Versuchsträgers (E-Kart) für Fahrerassistenzsysteme“.

I worked on an electric go-Kart that students previously worked on. My task was to collect measurement data from various sensors and 2 motor controllers installed on the e-Kart and send this data to a remote laptop.

For the transfer of data wirelessly I used 2 Xbee transceivers and Arduino UNO boards. For visualizing the data I played with 2 softwares but at the end I use LabVIEW to display all the information with gauges and graphs.

The collected data comes from the sensors embedded in the motor controllers plus 2 added sensors. Using CAN and CANopen protocols I was able to get the information I needed.
