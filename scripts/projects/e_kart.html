Telemetry E-kart

Spring 2017

This project was developed for my exchange university in Ingolstadt, Germany. The original title of the project is „Aufbau eines Versuchsträgers (E-Kart) für Fahrerassistenzsysteme“.

I worked on an electric go-Kart that students previously worked on. My task was to collect measurement data from various sensors and the 2 motor controllers installed on the e-Kart and send this data to a remote laptop.
